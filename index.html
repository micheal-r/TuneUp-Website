TuneUp Bot

The ultimate music game for your Discord server!

About TuneUp

TuneUp is a Discord bot designed to give you a fun experience on a Discord server. TuneUp will play your members music and they have to guess the song name, author and release date. With various gamemodes and songs you can surely enjoy TuneUp to the max!

Commands

/play [OPTIONAL: gamemode]

Start a game with your chosen gamemode.

/stop

Stop the current game and song.

/hint

Give a hint about the currently playing song.
